Festivals Celebrated at NavGurukul

Independence Day is celebrated with great enthusiasm. Students engage in cultural programs, flag hoisting, and sharing stories about India's freedom struggle.

During Holi, students celebrate by playing with colors, sharing sweets, and enjoying cultural activities that promote joy and togetherness.

Birthday celebrations at NavGurukul are filled with joy and bonding. The students organize surprise parties, cut cakes, and sing songs. The celebrations foster a strong sense of camaraderie among the students, making everyone feel special.

Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated with devotion. The campus is decorated, and students install a Ganesha idol, performing prayers and singing hymns. A sense of togetherness is felt during the celebrations, which include prasad distribution and cultural activities.

The Hackathon at NavGurukul is an exciting event where students collaborate in teams to solve real-world problems. The event fosters creativity, innovation, and teamwork. Participants work intensively for hours, and the best projects are recognized and awarded.

NavGurukul frequently hosts visitors from various industries who come to interact with students. These visits provide valuable exposure, allowing students to network and learn from experienced professionals. The visitors often conduct sessions to share insights and motivate the students.

The group photo captures the spirit of unity and friendship at NavGurukul. It symbolizes the journey of learning together, overcoming challenges, and building lifelong bonds. The smiles in the photo reflect the supportive environment that NavGurukul offers to its students.
